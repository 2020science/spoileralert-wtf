# Emerging Science and Technology

*Domain hub for spoileralert.wtf — based on Films from the Future by Andrew Maynard*

---

## About This Domain

Films from the Future explores a sweeping range of emerging sciences and technologies through the lens of twelve science fiction movies. This domain maps the key technologies and scientific concepts covered in the book, organized as individual topic pages that can be browsed, searched, and cross-referenced with the other domains on the site.

Each topic page introduces the technology or scientific concept, explains how the book uses a specific film to explore it, and connects it to real-world developments. The tone is accessible and engaging — this is not a textbook, but a guide for curious readers who want to understand what these technologies are and why they matter.

These technologies don't exist in isolation — they converge, interact, and amplify each other in ways that are often hard to predict. The cross-links on each topic page connect to ethical themes in the [Responsible and Ethical Innovation](/md-files/domain_responsible_and_ethical_innovation.md) domain and broader frameworks in the [Navigating the Future](/md-files/domain_navigating_the_future.md) domain.
